Quests

For the most part, Crossfire is about killing monsters. Players can kill monsters, gain experience and collect the loot. After a while though, this tends to get boring.

Quests are like the "salt in the soup". When a player gets involved in a task or plot which is more than just monster killing, that is a quest. Obviously quests are a must-have for any set of really good maps.

So how does one create a good quest? - There's no easy answer to this question. It's not only the plot, the storyline or the particular design which makes a quest good. It's the combination of everything.
